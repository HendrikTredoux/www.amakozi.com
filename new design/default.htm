Customer Requirements

Quest Software® needed to implement SDL Tridion 2009 to completely manage their marketing and support operations websites.

This involved implementing a new website design utilizing the latest Microsoft®.NET technologies and migrating existing content to the new system. The new content management system had to tightly integrate with systems such as Amdocs® Clarify CRM, the dedicated website indexing service provided by Endeca® and some proprietary Quest Software systems.
